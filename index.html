Game Tips

  • Pick up a glass first, then follow the step indicators on the order ticket.
  • Glowing stations show where to go next for your current drink.
  • Use the PASS counter (centre) to hand drinks between players.
  • Shake & Blend take ~1.5 seconds – plan ahead!
  • Faster service = bigger tips and bonus points.
